Microeconomics II Lecture Notes#

Course Introduction

Welcome to the learning journey of Microeconomics II!

This lecture note adopts an innovative teaching method, using stories and examples from daily life, especially “Grandma’s Chicken Farm” as the main metaphor, to help you understand the core concepts of microeconomics. We start from the most basic production theory and gradually go deeper into important topics such as cost analysis, pricing strategies, and market structure.

🎯 Learning Objectives#

This course will help you master:

  • Production Theory: Understand input-output relationships and production functions

  • Cost Analysis: Master various cost concepts and their application in decision-making

  • Market Structure: Analyze different market forms such as perfect competition and monopoly

  • Pricing Strategy: Learn how companies set optimal prices

  • Game Theory Basics: Understand strategic decision-making and information economics

📖 User Guide#

Learning Suggestions

  • Step-by-step: Please read in the order of chapters, as each concept builds on the previous foundation

  • Practical Application: Try to apply each economic concept to real-life scenarios

  • Focus on Understanding: Mathematical formulas are just analytical tools; the key is to understand the economic logic behind them

  • Interactive Learning: Use the dialogue format in each lecture to deepen your understanding of concepts
